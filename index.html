ðŸ§¬ What Is Neuroscience?

Neuroscience is the study of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. It helps us understand how we think, feel, move, and learn. Researchers use tools like EEG, fMRI, and MEG to observe brain activity and decode how neurons communicate.

ðŸ§ª What Is IONM?

Intraoperative Neuromonitoring (IONM) is a technique used during surgeries to monitor the nervous system in real time. It helps surgeons avoid damaging critical brain and spinal cord pathways.

  • Tracks motor and sensory signals during surgery
  • Alerts surgeons to potential nerve damage
  • Uses EEG, EMG, and evoked potentials like SSEP, MEP, and BERA
