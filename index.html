Intraoperative Neuromonitoring (IONM) is used during surgeries to monitor the nervous system in real time. It helps protect critical brain and spinal cord pathways.

  • Tracks motor and sensory signals
  • Alerts surgeons to nerve damage
  • Uses EEG, EMG, and evoked potentials
